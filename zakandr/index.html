Profile

I work as a multidisciplinary Creative Technologist across interactive product systems, 3D/WebGL experiences, AI-assisted workflows, visual identity, audio production, and digital publishing. My strongest work happens where several areas need to connect: software, product structure, interface logic, visual presentation, sound, and long-term creative direction.

I am not positioned as a narrow front-end developer only. Web development is one of the tools I use to build practical systems: configurators, browser-based product tools, interactive interfaces, visual experiences, SaaS-style workflows, and production pipelines that need both technical execution and taste.

A key example is LUNF.IO, my own SaaS product for furniture and storage companies. It combines interactive 3D configuration, product customization logic, customer request flow, admin-side order handling, website embedding, WordPress integration, and business-facing workflow logic.

My long-term music/media and writing projects are part of the same practice rather than a separate identity: they show audio engineering, sound design, visual identity, release presentation, editorial thinking, research systems, catalog organization, and independent production discipline.
